department-based eligibility validation system for a college portal.

Each student has a roll number (example: 23P81A6234).
The 7th and 8th characters of the roll number represent the department code.

DEPARTMENT CODES:
- 05 → Computer Science and Engineering [CSE]
- 69 → Computer Science and Engineering (IoT) [CSO]
- 04 → Electronics and Communication Engineering [ECE]
- 66 → Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning [CSM]
- 62 → Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) [CSC]
- 67 → Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) [CSD]

IMPORTANT:
- These rules apply for ALL years (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th year).
- Login is allowed for all students.
- Department rules apply ONLY when creating a team or selecting a department.

PRINCIPAL’S DEPARTMENT RULES:

1. CSE DEPARTMENT GROUP:
   - Allowed branches:
     - CSE (05)
     - CSO (69)
     - ECE (04)
   - Students from ONLY these branches can be part of the CSE department.
   - Students from CSM (66), CSC (62), CSD (67) are NOT allowed in CSE department.

2. CSM DEPARTMENT GROUP:
   - Allowed branches:
     - CSM (66)
     - CSC (62)
     - CSD (67)
     - ECE (04)
   - Students from ONLY these branches can be part of the CSM department.
   - Students from CSE (05), CSO (69) are NOT allowed in CSM department.

TEAM CREATION RULE:
- When a captain creates a team under a department (CSE or CSM):
  - Validate the captain’s department code from the roll number.
  - Allow team creation ONLY if the captain belongs to the allowed branch group.
  - Allow adding players ONLY if their department code is allowed under the same department group.
  - Reject any student who does not belong to the allowed branches.

TASK:
- Extract department code from roll numbers.
- Identify the selected department group (CSE or CSM).
- Validate eligibility strictly based on the rules above.
- Return one of the following responses:
  - "ELIGIBLE"
  - "NOT ELIGIBLE – DEPARTMENT RULE VIOLATION"
